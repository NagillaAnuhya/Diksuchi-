Introduction


Civil Engineering is one of the most rampant disciplines of Engineering. It is the oldest engineering discipline after Military Engineering. Cursively, from the word 'CIVIL' one can understand the meaning of it. It, roughly, means 'Construction is very important in Life'. But, if we have bird eye view on its application and careers, one can understand that, its not only limited to construction. It is also applied on the natural structures along with the construction of Dams, Bridges, Highways, Roads, Buildings, and Savages etc. For those, who want to know about career, it is experimented that jobs offered for civil Engineers have more perks and privileges than any other engineering jobs. It must be memorised that, it is possible, that might be your first job will not have the most perks privileges. In fact, your first job, might be, too hard with low compensation but this job experience and practice will surely lead you to a successful life.

Higher studies


There are multiple courses which candidates can pursue after completing the B.Tech civil course. These are mainly MTech programs generally ranging for 2 years. Check them below.

Master of Design (M.Des)


This degree deals with conceptualization and designing of the industrial requirement. This required good logical skills to provide creative and logical solutions to the problems affecting the environment and life.


Master of Building Engineering and Management (MBEM)


This involves building engineering concepts and providing scheduling and other techniques to manage it.


Master of Business Administration (MBA)


This is a diploma course that provides the knowledge of industrial engineering and management.


Master in Architecture (M.Arch)


This involves creating architecture or framework or design with viewpoints for industries. This involves creating housing plans, industrial designs, urban designs, etc.


Master of Planning


This deals with planning of environment and transport.


Master in Technology (M.Tech)


This involves structural engineering, study of CAD systems, CAD systems with structural analysis.

Average salary


Civil Engineers are responsible for the maintenance of the built environment - roads, railways, canals, highways, etc. Civil engineering is one of the most sought-after careers in India. The average base salary of civil engineers in India is around INR 25,000 per month and INR 3,07,507 per annum. This salary, however, tends to differ on the basis of experience. On average, the starting salary after becoming a civil engineer in India is around INR 2,42,000 per annum and can rise to about INR 8,62,000 per annum. The salary also differs on the basis of location. For example, on average, civil engineers in the United States earn around INR 46,36,252, quite more than civil engineers in India. The salary of a civil engineer also depends on the civil engineering courses a candidate has pursued as higher education will grant a higher salary.
