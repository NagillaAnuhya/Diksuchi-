METALLURGICAL ENGINEERING

Real gold is not afraid of melting pot.

Introduction


Metallurgical engineering involves the extraction of metals from their ore, and making them suited for practical use. Metals need to be got from their respective ores. The second step is to process the metals to make alloys, which is done according to the requirement and application.Metallurgy is the domain of materials science and engineering. The physical and chemical behavior of metallic elements, intermetallic compounds, and mixtures called alloys are studied in this bench of engineering. Metallurgy is the branch of engineering studying the science and the technology of metals.

Higher study options can be pursued either from Indian Universities or with international Universities.
