AERONAUTICAL ENGINEERING

It's not rocket science...oh, it actually is.

Introduction


Aeronautical Engineering is a well-known branch of engineering that attracts students with interest in airplanes and their mechanism. The primary job of an Aeronautical Engineer is to devise aircraft and propulsion systems, but with time, the engineer is given many more responsibilities to carry out. Aeronautical Engineer is providing direction and coordination to the designing, testing, and manufacturing of a spacecraft, aircraft, rocket system, space-shuttles, etc.Meet the quality standard, goals, design method, and completion dates.Aeronautical Engineer is the person who deals with the designing, testing, manufacturing, and constructing an aircraft.

Career options


  • Aerodynamics Engineer
  • Design Engineer
  • Engineering Consultant
  • Buyers and Procurement Officer
  • CNC programmers for the aerospace industry

Higher studies


The candidates can go for higher studies after B.Tech in Aeronautical Engineering. The candidates can go for their degree in aeronautical engineering. Some of the post graduate courses are: 1.Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Aerospace Engineering 2. Master of Engineering (M.E) in Aeronautical Engineering The candidates can also go for MBA programs. To do the post graduate programs, the aspirants have to appear for the entrance examinations like GATE, CAT, MAT, TANCET, etc. The candidate can choose the post graduate degree according to their wish.

Average salary


Aeronautical Engineer Salaries in India is one of the highest paid jobs in India as compared to the other related Engineer. Aeronautical Engineer Salaries depends on the organisation/company where employees are working but in the top organization Aeronautical Engineer salaries are excellent. Aeronautical Engineer at NASA, DRDO, Airlines earns a great salary and respect. Aeronautical Engineer:- The average salary package for the Aeronautical Engineer ranges from INR 7 to 8 lakhs per annum. Design Engineer:- The design engineer is to design the aircraft within Earth’s atmosphere such as airplanes, helicopters, etc. The average salary package lies from INR 4 to 5 lakhs per annum.
