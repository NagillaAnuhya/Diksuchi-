BIOTECHNOLOGY ENGINEERING

Biotechnology is the science of our future.

Introduction


Biotechnology engineering is the fusion of both biology and technology for the research and development of new products. It is an engineering discipline—the field of chemical engineering and applied biology—that includes engineering living organisms. It involves studying micro-organisms, bio-organisms and cell functions in living beings.Biotechnology engineering is a college degree program in applied sciences that amalgamates the realities from both Biological sciences and innovation. This study uses biological procedures which incorporate the investigation of microorganisms or information on antibiotics and further execute them for different modern reasons.

Average salary


The average annual starting salary offered to BTech Biotechnology in India ranges between INR 5 and 7 Lakh depending on the candidate's skills and knowledge.The average biotechnology engineer gross salary in India is ₹13,06,683 or an equivalent hourly rate of ₹628. In addition, they earn an average bonus of ₹22,998. Salary estimates based on salary survey data collected directly from employers and anonymous employees in India. An entry level biotechnology engineer (1-3 years of experience) earns an average salary of ₹9,28,678. On the other end, a senior level biotechnology engineer (8+ years of experience) earns an average salary of ₹16,48,135.
