Introduction


BTech ECE is a 4-year program focused on technologies in critical areas such as telecommunications, electricity and electronics, while promoting the development of the skills required to combine hardware and software components.An ECE branch mainly deals with the communication and the electronics background with the involvement of the electrical knowledge. These do deal with the resistors, PCB’s, capacitors, bread boards, what not all the digital devices that are used in the modern world. Electronics and Communication Engineering” is one of the fastest growing field of engineering courses. It involves the transmission of information across the channel. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) compiles in Communication Engineering and core Electronics Engineering.

Higher studies


After completing B.Tech in Electronics and Communication, one can pursue M.E, M.Tech and PG Diploma courses in India as well as abroad. Those who opt for M.E/M.Tech/PG Diploma courses offered in India can explore areas of Digital Systems Engineering, Microwave and Radar Engineering, Industrial Electronics etc. M.E/M.Tech/PG Diploma courses offered at foreign Universities cover more advanced areas like Digital Media Engineering, Sensors and Sensing Systems and Industrial Electronics and Control.
